How to Preserve Tomatoes Properly

  • Store ripe tomatoes at room temperature, away from direct sunlight.
  • Once ripe, refrigerate them to slow down further ripening.
  • To extend freshness, freeze tomatoes for later use in sauces or soups.
  • Keep tomatoes separated from other fruits to prevent ethylene gas exposure.
  • For long-term storage, can tomatoes with seasonings for homemade sauces.
